About Us

Our story, our people, our values.

Our Beginnings

Born from Passion

Kalayaan Cafe was founded in 2024, by coffee enthusiasts who wanted to create a space that felt like home — warm, welcoming, and deeply Filipino.

The name Kalayaan (Filipino for "freedom") reflects their vision: a place where people are free to just be — to work, to dream, to rest, or simply enjoy a good cup of coffee without rushing.

What started as a tiny 20-seater cafe on a side street in Rizal has grown into a beloved community hub, serving thousands of cups every month while staying true to its roots.
