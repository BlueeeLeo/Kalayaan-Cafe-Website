Our Story

Brewed with Passion,
Served with Heart

Kalayaan Cafe was born from a love of good coffee and Filipino community. Nestled in the heart of Quezon City, we believe every cup should feel like a small celebration of freedom — kalayaan.

Our beans are sourced from local farms in Benguet, Sagada, and Mt. Apo — supporting Filipino farmers while delivering exceptional flavor.
